My long term goals

Goals can be hard to think about sometimes. However, I know what I want to achieve.

One work place goal is diversity! In the tech industry, it is clear that there are some diversity issues. Tech lacks difference. Tech only has 15% of its workforce coming from a BAME background. Only 19% of workers are women. Even so, 42% of women in tech state that there are a lack of role models for them. So how can one woman make a difference without just becoming another stat? I think it would be important for the companies in tech to be open to employee ideas. Companies should look into programmes that promote diversity in the tech sector. For example, a company called Code First Girls specialises in promoting and teaching coding to girls and women. Other companies such as Heroworx offer bootcamps to help people learn - free of charge! On a more personal level, my main goal in life is to do something meaningful. So let's make a change together.
